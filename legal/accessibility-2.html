Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: Friday March 8, 2025

Reformer LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. We continuously strive to improve the user experience and apply the relevant accessibility standards to enhance usability for everyone.

‍Our CommitmentWe aim to comply with the

‍Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level, which provides recommendations for making web content more accessible to people with various disabilities.

‍Accessibility Features

‍To support accessibility, we have implemented:
• Alternative text for images and non-text content
• Keyboard navigability throughout our website
• Sufficient color contrast for readability
• Captioning and transcripts for multimedia content
• A focus indicator for interactive elements

‍Ongoing Efforts We regularly audit our website and applications to identify and fix accessibility barriers. Our team is trained on accessibility best practices and works to integrate them into our digital products.
